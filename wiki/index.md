## ⚠️ Most Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Skipping the move-in inspection — you may be charged for damage you did not cause
- Not getting everything in writing — verbal agreements are very hard to prove
- Missing the move-out notice deadline — your lease may auto-renew
- Assuming the landlord's insurance covers your belongings — it does not
